Our Story

The journey began in a small university campus where three students, Emma, Raj, and Lisa, observed massive food waste. Driven by the desire to bridge the gap between waste and hunger, they created this app to efficiently redistribute surplus food.

Our Vision & Mission

To foster a sustainable ecosystem where surplus food is redistributed efficiently, reducing waste and hunger while promoting environmental responsibility.
